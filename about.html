Hi There!

I’m Jeffrey Su. I am a user centric designer focusing on marginalised groups and accessibility. I have a passion for HCI and gamified interfaces, and love learning more about how users interact with products and interfaces. I create speculative designs based on human interaction principles, applying my hybrid skill set in design, code, and material fabrication to create meaningful solutions to problems.

I am a hobbyist at heart, a foodie by nature, and infatuated with film photography. If I'm not out exploring new restaurants, you can find me online chipping away at my backlog of games.
