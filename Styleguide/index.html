Branding Basics

Who am I to potential employers?

I am a user centric designer focusing on marginalised groups and accessibility. I have a passion for HCI and gamified interfaces, and love learning more about how users interact with products and interfaces. I create speculative designs based on human interaction principles, applying my hybrid skill set in design, code, and material fabrication to create solutions for wicked problems.I am a UX designer aiming to showcase my work with HCI and user interation principles. My portfolio follows branding that makes use of texture and minimal coloured accents to draw attention to elements. My motif is that of a notebook, connecting back to my background in material fabrication as well as my passion for film photography.

I enjoy making pleasing yet usable designs. As a designer I believe that my page, as a designer, should be elegantly presented and highlight my ability to command layout and balance of the page .

For the typefaces, PP mori allowed for a straightforward and easy to read experience. I felt that a simple sans serif like PP Mori would represent my designs and my personality well. Headings being Italicied help diferentiate itself from the hierarchy, and combined with the lighter coloured h2 also in PP Mori it separates the hierarchy well. PP mori is simple yet has a hint of character which diferentiates itself from other san serifs.

Going into usability, System fonts should be used if the above mentioned typefaces are not available or cannot be injected in the markup. Choosing from the list of system typefaces available, Arial italics for h1, verdana regular for h2 and Arial regular for body copy are the most similar to the typefaces above. They are quite similar, however lack the character PP Mori provides. However, they still have similar qualities and funtion the same roles.

I like to focus on colour and texture in my work. This is no different in my portfolio. My background is made of a paperlike texture, making use of a noise pattern. This is reminicient of film photography of which inspires some of my work. Colour wise, It is quite simple, leaning into whites, greys, and blacks with loud pops of coloured accents . These accents are for CTA and help users learn of the site patterns and aids them in intelligence, using simple semiotics to denote interactable elements.
